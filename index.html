Our Vision

Being the most successful School of Turkey.

Our Mission

Raising students who are creative, smart, good in human relations, in peace with themselfs and others, aware of the universal , national, cultural, moral values and talking at least one foreign language.

Our Values

In accordance with the Fundamental Turkish Education intents and principles,

  • Appling an understanding of education which embraces Ataturk’s principles and revolutions.
  • Teaching with the sympathy of scientific principles under the leadership of science.
  • Supporting students who have difficulties in academic and social means.
  • Making students accept that education is a life long process.
